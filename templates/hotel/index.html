Cascade Ski Club and Lodge

Committed to preserving affordable, accessible, year-round mountain experiences centered around our lodge, fostering fun and friendship for families, youth, and mountain enthusiasts of all ages.

Our Roots

  • Founded in 1928 by Norwegian ski jumpers, Cascade Ski Club is one of America's oldest ski clubs. Located in the heart of Government Camp, the club's Grand Lodge offers a traditional gathering place for ski and snowboard enthusiasts, racers, and families.

  • The Lodge provides members with easy access to mountain activities like skiing, snowboarding, tubing, hiking, fishing, mountain biking, and climbing. It offers affordable dorm-style bunks, hot meals on weekends, modern facilities, and a cozy atmosphere for members and their families, as well as organized groups (by reservation). Guests can visit if accompanied by a member. -->
